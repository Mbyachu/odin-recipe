Lasagna

ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until softened.
  2. Add ground beef to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and sugar. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
